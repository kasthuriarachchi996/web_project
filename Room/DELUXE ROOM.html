Early Arrivals & Late Departures


Arrivals before 10:00am need to be booked & paid for from the previous night. Early departure, during peak occupancy is subject to 100% charge on the remaining room. Late departures incur 50% charge up to 06:00pm & a full day’s charge thereafter.


Guaranteed Reservations & Cancellation Policy


Guaranteed reservations will be charged for the full reservation by credit card provided. Cancellations made 24 hours prior to arrival are free of charge, any cancellations made after are subject to one night’s charge.


Family Plan


Rooms accommodate maximum three adults per room or two adults & two children (under 12 years). Children under 12 will not be charged for sharing the same room. Children aged six to 12 will be charged 50% of meal charges & children below six are offered free meals.
