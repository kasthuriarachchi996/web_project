About Us

With over twenty years of experience hosting travellers in the city of Matara, Sea Side Hotels Group have learnt a thing or two about those visiting the city. You make a plan either business or pleasure, We will cater you accordingly. .

Experience the beauty of the Indian Ocean from Sea Side Hotel. Ride the rolling waves of Mathara. Embrace of the salty breeze on the sandy beach shaded by the coconut grove. Treat your eyes to beautiful seascapes that fill the soul with tranquility. Savour the delicious flavours of fusion cuisine. Let the tunes of the night keep you upbeat and flying high

Feel at home

There is always a friendly face around to make you feel pampered and right at home

Experience

You also need to be ready for whatever the day will bring you so we offer you free wi-fi, tea and coffee facilities in your room and almost all of our hotels provide a hearty breakfast to set you up for the day. As with most hotels in the city, rooms are on the smaller side, but this means you'll get unbeatable value and won't be charged for unnecessary frills that you won't be using.

Service

And for all those last minute requests that inevitably crop up, our reception and concierge staff always have a plan to help. From collecting train tickets to the best local dinner haunts, every member of Sea side Hotels Group is here to make your trip as smooth as possible.
